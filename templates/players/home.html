Capture the flag (CTF) is a kind of information security competition that challenges contestants to solve, exploit a various range of tasks which includes web, cryptography, OSINT, steganography, forensic, networking, reverse engineering etc. After successfully solving a challenge/task players will get a piece of text which is called a flag. Then they can submit these flags into the site to check if they solved the challenge successfully or not. If players submit the correct flag, then they will get a certain amount of point. After the end of the competition the player with most of the point wins.
